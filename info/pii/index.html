Be careful about your PII

Even seemingly insignificant pieces of PII (personal information) can be used to identify you. If you leave an anonymous comment saying that you drive a red Honda, it's not enough to identify you. However in another comment you mention that you're 45, then 3 months later you say that you work for a small retailer store. Someone can put all this together, look at when you usually post comments to determine your timezone and then make a good guess who you are. Fragmentation of information is your friend. If you've slipped too much PII, change accounts and make it difficult to build your profile.
