Now anybody can post comments, but you can choose to show only a subset of those comments. Just like anyone has the right to say what they think about your page, you have the right to not show any of those comments on your page. Read more about comntr filters.

The iframe on the right uses a filter id that I have a ed25519 private key for. This means that while anybody can add comments, I can ban some of them. You can remove the filter id, or use the browser extension, to see all the comments, even those that I chose to ban, or you can create your own filter id, put it on your page and ban comments you don't like.
